# Send His Love to Me

[Verse 1]
Lover had to leave me 'cross the desert plain
Turned to me, his lady, tell me, "Lover, wait"

[Chorus]
Calling Jesus, please
Send his love to me

[Verse 2]
Oh, wind and rain, they haunt me, look to the north and pray
Send me, please, his kisses, send them home today

[Chorus]
I'm begging, Jesus, please
Send his love to me

[Verse 3]
Left alone in desert, this house becomes a hell
This love becomes a tether, this room becomes a cell

[Chorus]
Mummy, daddy, please
Send him back to me

[Verse 4]
How long must I suffer? Dear God, I've served my time
This love becomes my torture, this love, my only crime
Oh, lover, please release me, my arms too weak to grip
My eyes too dry for weeping, my lips too dry to kiss

[Chorus]
Calling Jesus, please
Send his love to me
I'm begging, Jesus, please
Send his love to me
